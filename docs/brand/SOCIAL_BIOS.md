## Voice & tone notes (apply across channels)

- **Confident, direct, grounded.** Avoid hype words ("revolutionary", "next-gen", "game-changing").
- **Technical when appropriate** (builders + validators) — concrete numbers, code snippets, version pins.
- **Institutional when appropriate** (LinkedIn, partner channels) — formal but not stiff. No "we're stoked" or "we crushed it".
- **Indonesian context welcome** — but English primary across all official channels until specifically localizing.
- **No specific RWA verticals** in social copy until signed partnerships materialize. Generic "real-world assets" / "real economy".
- **Avoid competitive framing** — don't punch at Ethereum/Solana/Avalanche. Position by what Sentrix IS, not what it's against.
