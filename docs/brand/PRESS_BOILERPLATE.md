## Long version (200 words — features/profile pieces)

```
About Sentrix Chain

Sentrix Chain is a Layer-1 blockchain purpose-built for the real
economy, starting with Indonesia. Founded by Satya Kwok and built in
Rust as a fourteen-crate workspace, Sentrix combines the monetary
discipline that made Bitcoin durable — fixed 315M supply with Bitcoin-
parity 4-year halving — with the developer ergonomics that made
Ethereum the dominant smart-contract platform. The chain runs Voyager
DPoS+BFT consensus with EVM-native execution (revm 37), enabling
existing Solidity tooling to deploy without modification.

Sentrix is designed for tokenization of real-world assets — property,
trade settlement, fixed-income instruments, and beyond. The chain
launched mainnet in April 2026 with four validators and has operated
continuously since.

The company behind the chain, SentrisCloud, is building products and
operational tooling on top of the protocol. Sentrix Chain itself is
licensed under BUSL-1.1 and transitions to open-source after the
Change Date. The protocol is community-anchored: stake-weighted
validator selection (15,000 SRX self-stake floor), 50% transaction
fee burn (deflationary), and roadmap commitments to parallel execution
(Frontier phase) and cross-chain interoperability (Odyssey phase).

Website:  sentrixchain.com (chain & dev tools), sentriscloud.com (company & products)
GitHub:   github.com/sentrix-labs (protocol), github.com/sentriscloud (products)
Press:    press@sentriscloud.com
Partners: partners@sentriscloud.com
```

## What NOT to say in press

❌ "Indonesian Bitcoin" — confused (Sentrix has smart contracts, BTC doesn't)
❌ "Faster than Ethereum" — race-to-the-bottom claim, ages poorly
❌ "Revolutionary blockchain" — generic hype
❌ "Web3 for everyone" — vague buzzword
❌ "Solana killer" / "Ethereum killer" — competitive framing without basis
❌ Specific RWA verticals (property, gold, invoices, bonds) until signed partnerships exist

## What TO say

✅ "Real economy" — central anchor of positioning
✅ "Indonesia first, Southeast Asia next, world after" — scope arc
✅ "Bitcoin's monetary discipline meets Ethereum's programmability" — tech parallel
✅ "315M fixed supply, 4-year halving (Bitcoin-parity)" — concrete numbers
✅ "Production since April 2026" — credibility marker
✅ "Sub-second finality via DPoS+BFT" — performance claim that's defensible
✅ "EVM-native, Solidity-ready" — developer accessibility
